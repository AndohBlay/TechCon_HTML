Our History

Since its inception in 2010, TechCon has been a pivotal gathering for tech enthusiasts, developers, entrepreneurs, and industry leaders. Over the years, it has evolved into a global platform for innovation and knowledge sharing.

Our Mission

TechCon’s mission is to inspire and connect the global tech community by showcasing transformative technologies, fostering innovation, and promoting collaboration. We believe in making technology accessible and impactful for everyone.

Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovecode – A renowned AI researcher who revolutionized machine learning frameworks. She spoke at TechCon 2020 on ethical AI development.

Linus Devson – Creator of OpenOS, Linus shared insights on open-source collaboration and future operating system designs at TechCon 2022.
